About

"Freely Dream Together, Boldly Challenge the Future"

TI:um is an officially recognized student honor society established by the School of Transdisciplinary Innovations, Seoul National University. It was founded through the full initiative of students and led by students from the first proposal to its official establishment in December 2025 with 20 members. It is now home to 20+ undergraduate members and fellows, 7+ past and present subgroups, 5+ collaborative institutions and student organizations, thanks to the full support of the school and inspiring members.

TI:um is a place of discovery, creativity, connection and innovation. Dedicated to our foundational missionвҖ”Freely Dream Together, Boldly Challenge the FutureвҖ”we are working to create innovations through benefiting society with various projects both on and beyond campus. From monthly academic seminars, open subgroup activities, international academic exchange programs, to social contribution projects in collaboration with the Hokkaido University, Japan, all of these various projects are created and carried out by students.

Students of TI:um freely cross the domains of technology, humanities, arts, and sciencesвҖ”connecting ideas flexibly in their intersections to create impactful solutions for the most important and challenging global problems. Through transdisciplinary approaches, TI:um provides fertile soil to let students sprout creative ideas and blossom sustainable innovations towards a better future.

An open and caring innovative community for those who challenge, a spring of passionate aspirations, and a heartfelt community that grows together toward a better future.

We are TI:um.
